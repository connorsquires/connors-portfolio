I’m Connor Squires, a product designer, outdoor enthusiast, collector of miles, Magic: The Gathering dabbler, and e-reader convert.

Also,

I love puzzles.

Business or pleasure, easy or challenging—if I don’t know it, you can bet I’m going to figure it out. I love diving into complex systems and piecing together how everything works. Whether it leads to a killer design or a newfound love of woodcarving, I’m all in.

I believe collaboration brings life to design.

This is where the magic happens. I’ve worked both solo and on teams, and while I can get things done on my own, I thrive in a team environment. There’s something electric about bouncing ideas around, pushing through creative disagreements, and building something together that’s bigger than any one person’s vision. It’s messy, fun, and always worth it.
